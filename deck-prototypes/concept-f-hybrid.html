ACT VI
THE NEW PLAYBOOK
The Principle
Design Workflows for Agents Like You Design Them for Humans
A PM workflow today: gather signal, synthesize context, draft spec, align stakeholders, ship decision. Each step can be an agent. The manager becomes the orchestrator.
Before
Manager assigns tasks to 8 humans. Coordinates via meetings. Reviews output manually.
After
Manager orchestrates 8 agents. Coordinates via prompts. Reviews output with evals.
The Shift
"Everyone is becoming a generalist."
When AI handles the depth, humans provide the breadth. The one-person billion-dollar startup is no longer a meme. It is the trajectory.
Action 1
AI Budget
Allocate a monthly budget to experiment with AI tools. Treat it as an investment.
Action 2
Build AI Fluency
Use AI tools daily. Not just ChatGPT. Learn prompting, agents, evaluations.
Action 3
Think in Workflows
Break your work into steps. Identify which steps agents can own entirely.
Action 4
Become a Generalist
Let AI handle the depth. You provide the breadth, the judgment, the taste.
