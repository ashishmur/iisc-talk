## Behance Analysis: Key Findings

### Per-Deck Highlights
1. **Paradise Travel**: Dark/premium, orange gradient accent, serif+sans pairing, full-bleed photo with dark overlays, botanical line art motif
2. **Clean Minimal Yellow**: White bg, mustard accent, single sans-serif, 60%+ whitespace per slide, extreme restraint (2-3 elements/slide)
3. **I Sell Drugs** (Ashish's favorite): Black/white only + rainbow accent, typography-driven (50-80% slide width), organic hand-drawn oval outlines as motif, single idea per slide, 70%+ negative space on statement slides
4. **Nexora AI**: Dark navy, orange 3D spheres, orbital ring diagrams, Inter font, cinematic product mockups
5. **X Template**: Swiss/modernist, B&W + red accent, diagonal X-cut motif dividing slides, full-bleed B&W photography
6. **Eluma Portfolio**: Single vibrant orange as entire identity, .01/.02 numbering, starburst motif, both light and dark modes

### Synthesized Design Principles (Top 10)
1. **Typography is the hero** - Dual-font: geometric sans for structure + italic serif for emotion
2. **Max 2-3 working colors** - One neutral base + one defining accent
3. **50-70% whitespace per slide** - Whitespace IS the design
4. **Dark mode dominance** - Creates automatic premium perception
5. **Consistent structural elements** - Repeating "furniture" on every slide (logo, numbering, margin frame)
6. **One idea per slide** - If >1 headline, break into two slides
7. **Treated photography** - Overlays, B&W, geometric crops (never raw stock)
8. **Mix slide density** - Oscillate between breathing slides and working slides
9. **Recurring geometric motif** - One signature shape threading through all slides
10. **Visible navigation** - Slide numbering (.01, .02), table of contents, section markers
