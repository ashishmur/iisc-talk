What You Should Do

01
AI Budget
Dedicate time and money to AI tools. Treat it as essential infrastructure, not a nice-to-have.
02
Build AI Fluency
Use AI daily. Prompt, iterate, evaluate. Build muscle memory for working with agents.
03

Map your processes. Find where AI agents can slot in. Design for delegation.
04
Become a Generalist
Learn everything. The polymath wins. AI handles depth; you provide breadth and judgment.
