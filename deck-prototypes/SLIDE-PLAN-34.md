# 34-Slide Plan: AI & The New Management Playbook

## ACT I: THE TENSION
1. **Title**: "AI & The New Management Playbook" / Ashish Muralidharan / Product Manager, Microsoft / IISc DoMS, March 6, 2026
2. **Opening Poll**: "How many of you feel anxious about your future, your career, your relevance, because of AI?"
3. **Section Divider**: ACT I - THE TENSION
4. **VUCA Framework**: Volatility, Uncertainty, Complexity, Ambiguity. "It's a one-way street going upwards."
5. **The Pattern of Disruption**: Shipping > Industrial Revolution > Information Technology > Artificial Intelligence
6. **The Economic Equation**: Land, Labor, Capital, Technology. "Technology is fundamentally replacing labor."

## ACT II: THE PATTERN
7. **Section Divider**: ACT II - THE PATTERN
8. **Technology vs. Labor Cycle**: 4 steps: Tech arrives > Physical displaced > Operational displaced > Intellectual displaced
9. **Running Out of Rungs** (bars): Physical 95% / Operational 72% / Intellectual 45% / ??? 15%
10. **The Ceiling Quote**: "If you don't need intellectual labor anymore, what's left?"
11. **Robots & The Physical Frontier**: Tesla Optimus, Unitree, Hyundai. "Complete replacement across every dimension."
12. **5 Levels of AGI** (table): L1 Conversational (done) / L2 Reasoning (done) / L3 Agents (HERE) / L4 AI Orgs (emerging) / L5 AGI (unknown)
13. **What Is An Agent?**: "A system that can pursue a goal by reasoning, planning, using tools, and continuously learning."

## ACT III: GROUND ZERO - SOFTWARE
14. **Section Divider**: ACT III - GROUND ZERO
15. **Why Software First?**: 100% digital / Signal-to-closure fully automated / Rich training data
16. **The New Reality**: "Single engineers spin up 20 agents that build the entire product end-to-end."
17. **Voices from the Field**: Karpathy: "vibe coding" / Naval: "PM is vibe coding. Engineering is prompt engineering."
18. **Cost to Build**: "The cost to build software has come down to zero."
19. **The New Battle**: Distribution & marketing. "The new job for everyone has become marketing."
20. **Real Consequences**: Block: 50% layoffs / IT stocks down 25-30% / Innovator's dilemma playing out in real-time

## ACT IV: THE ECONOMICS
21. **Section Divider**: ACT IV - THE ECONOMICS
22. **The Abundance Thesis**: Remove humans from process > Deflation > Goods cheaper > Era of abundance
23. **The AI Value Chain**: Energy > Chips > Fabricators > Frontier Labs > Hyperscalers > App Layer > Distribution
24. **Where Value Lives**: Not the model. Compute + Context + Distribution. "Agents don't need UI."

## ACT V: FROM THE INSIDE
25. **Section Divider**: ACT V - FROM THE INSIDE
26. **Microsoft's AI Journey**: Top-down failed. Bottom-up worked. Unlimited compute + incentive alignment = 20x productivity.
27. **Project Nebula**: 100 PMs, 600 engineers, 40-50 designers. Weekly AI upskilling. "Tribal knowledge."
28. **The Learning**: "The most valuable managers will be the ones who figure out how to diffuse AI throughout the company."

## ACT VI: THE NEW PLAYBOOK
29. **Section Divider**: ACT VI - THE NEW PLAYBOOK
30. **AI Organizations**: Design workflows for agents like managers designed them for humans. PM workflow example.
31. **Two Skills**: 1. Prompting (instruct precisely) / 2. Evaluations (measure AI quality)
32. **The Age of the Polymath**: "Everyone is becoming a generalist." The one-person billion-dollar startup.
33. **What You Should Do** (4-action grid): AI Budget / Build AI Fluency / Think in Workflows / Become a Generalist

## CLOSING
34. **Closing**: Return to anxiety > "Go build something."
